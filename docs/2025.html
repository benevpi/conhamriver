Conham 2025: E. coli, CSO spilling, rain & temperature

Weekly E. coli samples and the CSO spilling on the sample day and in the prior 2 and 7 days, over daily rainfall and mean temperature, May–December 2025. Wind isn't in the fetched data yet — see the note below.

E. coli is capped at 1000 CFU/100ml (the lab maximum, dashed line). CSO = summed hours of upstream spilling on the sample day and in the 2 and 7 days before it (each panel on its own scale — note the differing y-axes). Rain & temperature are ERA5 daily values for Conham. Hover for values. Source: docs/data/conham_2025_timeseries.csv.
